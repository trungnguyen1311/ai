# Product Plan: Union Officer Profile Management (MVP)

## 1. Business Goals
- **Digitalization**: Move from paper-based or scattered spreadsheets to a centralized digital profile for each Union Officer.
- **Self-Service**: Empower officers to maintain their own personal contact information, reducing administrative burden.
- **Accuracy**: Ensure the central database has up-to-date contact details for communication.

## 2. Target Audience
- **Primary**: Union Officers (Cán bộ công đoàn).
- **Secondary**: System Admin (future scope, out of scope for this MVP).

## 3. User Stories (MVP)
| ID | As a... | I want to... | So that... |
|----|---------|--------------|------------|
| US-1 | Union Officer | View my full profile details | I can verify the information held about me is correct. |
| US-2 | Union Officer | Edit my contact information (Phone) | The union can contact me when necessary. |
| US-3 | Union Officer | Edit my residential address | My records are kept current. |
| US-4 | Union Officer | View my official Union positions | I can confirm my current standing and tenure details. |

## 4. Feature List (User-Facing)
### A. Profile Dashboard / Overview
- Display of basic identity information (Name, Avatar, Employee ID/Union ID).
- Summary of current position/role.

### B. Personal Information Management
- **View**: Full Name, Date of Birth, Gender, ID Card Number (CCCD/CMND).
- **Edit**:
    - Phone Number
    - Email Address (Personal)
    - Residential Address

### C. Union Professional Details (Read-Only)
- Display of immutable official data:
    - Union Position (e.g., Chairman, Committeeman).
    - Unit/Department.
    - Union Join Date.
    - Party Member Status (Yes/No).

## 5. Success Metrics
- % of active users who have visited their profile page.
- % of users who have updated their contact info at least once.
